Early access info: 
Hey everyone — quick (but important) heads-up about **Early Access gamepasses** and how we’re rolling out new classes.

### Why Early Access will be **by phases**, and only **2 classes per phase**

I’m going to release classes in **phases**, and in each phase there will be **only 2 classes available in Early Access** through a gamepass. When a new phase drops, it comes with a **new Early Access gamepass** for that phase’s classes.

Why do it like this?

If I made **one single “Early Access for every future class”** pass, it would be cool for a moment… and then it would basically kill the game’s ability to fund itself long-term. After a while, I’d stop making money from new class releases, and that’s bad for development, updates, servers, assets, everything.

This phased model is the most realistic way for me to:

* **keep the game funded over time**
* **keep shipping new classes consistently**
* and still keep things fair, because…

### Yes: classes will be **FREE after Early Access**

Early Access is **temporary**. After the Early Access period ends, the classes from that phase will become **free for everyone**.
So you’re not paying to “own the class forever” — you’re paying to **play it earlier** and support the game while it’s being built.

### Anti Pay-to-Win rule for upcoming phases

Also, starting in the next phases (and this already includes the **new power from Phase 1**), the **level cap will be Level 10 during Early Access**.

That’s on purpose:

* to avoid **pay-to-win**
* to keep the fights fair
* and to make sure Early Access feels like **early testing + hype**, not “buy power and delete everyone”

---

If you buy Early Access, you’re basically saying: “I want to support development and get in early.”
If you don’t, you’ll still get the classes later for free — no FOMO trap.

More details soon when Phase 1 drops. Appreciate you all for real.

# 📋 **Server General Information: Appeal Center**
Welcome to the **Appeal Center**. This server is dedicated to maintaining a fair environment for our community by providing a streamlined system to handle appeals, reports, and staff-related issues.

Our goal is to ensure that every player is heard and that all moderation actions are reviewed with transparency.

### 🛠️ **How our Support System Works**
We utilize an automated ticket system to keep your data private and organized. To receive help:
* Navigate to the <#create-a-ticket channel.
* Select the button that matches your issue.
* A private channel will be created for you to communicate with our team.

### 📂 **Support Categories**
### 🖥️ ***Server Support***
Use this category if you are facing issues within the Discord server itself.
* **Appealing Warnings/Mutes:** If you believe a server-side punishment was unjustified.
* **Appealing Server Bans: **For users who have been removed from the Discord.
* **Reporting a Member:** To report a community member for breaking Discord rules.

### 🎮 ***Game Support (Roblox)***
Use this category for all issues related to the in-game experience.
* **Appealing Game Bans:** If you have been banned from our Roblox experience.
* **Reporting Players:** To report hackers, glitch-abusers, or individuals ruining the game experience.
* **Format Required: **Please ensure you have your Roblox Username and video/photo evidence ready before opening these tickets.

### ⚖️ ***File a Complaint (Supervisor Only)***
This is a high-level category handled exclusively by our **Supervisor Team**.
* Use this ONLY for filing formal complaints regarding the conduct or actions of a **Staff Member**.
*Note:* Misuse of this category may lead to a suspension of ticket privileges.

### ⏱️ **Ticket Policies**
To ensure our team can help everyone efficiently, please keep the following in mind:
* **Formatting:** When you open a ticket, a specific format will be provided. Please fill it out completely.
* **Inactivity:** Tickets with no activity for 24 hours will receive a warning. If a ticket remains inactive for 48 hours, our system will automatically close and archive it.
* **Transcripts:** Once a ticket is closed, a full transcript is logged for security and record-keeping. You may receive a copy of this via DM (if your DMs are open).
* **Feedback:** After your ticket is resolved, you will have the option to rate your experience from 1 to 5 stars. We value this feedback to improve our staff team.

Behavior: Staff are expected to demonstrate decent behavior to best represent the PM Team and to ensure that staff can work one-on-one in a friendly environment of mutual respect. Members of staff are recruited based not just on their ability, but equally on their personality. Members should feel welcomed by staff members and find them approachable.
Teamwork: While a Staff member at PM, you should regularly collaborate with fellow staff members to help us maintain an informed staff team.
Dedication: Staff should be dedicated to committing time to moderating the server and assisting community members. Staff are recruited based on their drive to support PM in its goals and growth. Inactivity in moderation stats can get you striked.
BIAS: In your position as a staff member, it’s expected that you will remain impartial when performing moderation duties. Staff found to be working with bias will face discipline or possible termination from their position. An impartial team relates to the behavior of staff.
Moderation: When moderating the server, staff are expected to follow the moderation system to the best of their ability unless the system is not appropriate or applicable to a certain situation. Staff should remain professional when issuing warnings, mutes, kicks, and bans, following the given formats to prevent confrontation with community members and claims of abuse.
Confidentiality: Information given to staff outside of public channels is expected to remain secret and should not be leaked—likewise with discussions held in staff channels. Staff who are found to have leaked or distributed staffing material to non-staff will face immediate termination.
Discord TOS: Staff are required to comply with Discord’s Terms of Service. Staff found to be disregarding Discord’s ToS could face immediate termination. (https://discord.com/terms)
Breaking these conditions could result in a staff strike or could possibly be grounds for demotions
Content which should be moderated includes, but is not limited to: 18+/other NSFW content, slur usage, spam, doxxed information, users which are being hostile and/or argumentative, self, and non-self, advertisement, and impersonation of staff.

Moderators reserve the right to moderate at their own discretion however must not moderate with bias. Moderators found moderating with bias or abusing their powers will, depending on circumstance, receive a strike or be removed from the staff team.

When moderating content, the type of action you take should depend on the severity of the content in question. PK has a system in place which helps determine the action that you take. This system should be followed by all members of staff, HR included. 

Moderation system:

Warn 1 - verbal warning issued at discretion of moderators 
Warn 2 - logged warning issued using ‘?warn’ command 
Warn 3 - mute user(s) in question using ‘?mute’ command; include time period
Warn 4 - logged warning issued using ‘?warn’ command or mute if appropriate 
Warn 5 - auto-ban; whether it is appealable or not will depend on ban reason(s)

The moderation system will not always be applicable to every situation. Depending on the severity of the situation in question, moderators may directly mute or ban a user. e.g. a member that’s posting NSFW content should be banned regardless of their warnings if the content in question is severe or is considered to be 18+ content.

PK does not moderate content said or sent in DMs or in game. Please direct those who report users for something said in DMs or in game to Discord or Roblox support.
