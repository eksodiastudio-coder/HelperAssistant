# PROJECT EVOLVERS: COMPREHENSIVE DATA

## [GAMEPLAY & CLASSES]
- Game Name: Project: Evolvers (Inspired by Control, Push, Dark Minds, Stranger Things).
- Core Concept: PVP + RP. Players are experiments training to unlock powers.
- Movers Class (Free):
  * Lvl 5: Bone break & Leap
  * Lvl 10: Levitation
  * Lvl 15: Shockwave
  * Lvl 20: Pull
  * Lvl 25: Shield
- Igniters Class: Currently in Early Access (Phase 1).
- Leveling: Kills are the fastest way to level. 
- Gamepasses: 
  * 2x Energy (400 Robux): Helps get more kills.
  * 2x XP (500 Robux): More XP per kill; fewer kills needed to level.
  * Igniters Early Access (350 Robux): Play it early; will be FREE later.

## [EARLY ACCESS PHILOSOPHY]
- Phased Releases: Classes drop in phases (2 classes per phase).
- Reason: Phasing ensures long-term funding for development, servers, and assets.
- Fairness: All Early Access classes become FREE for everyone after the testing phase ends.
- Anti-P2W Rule: Level cap is restricted to Level 10 during Early Access to keep fights fair.
